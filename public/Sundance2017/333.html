- A rural Ohio eccentric with an obsession for 'Stradivari' convinces a famous European concert violinist that he can make a copy of one of the most famous and valuable violins in the world. Fighting time, poverty, and most of all - himself - Danny Houck puts everything on the line for one shot at glory. Cast: Daniel Houck, Razvan Stoica, David Campbell, Rodger Stearns, Mary Houck.
