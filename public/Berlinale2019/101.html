- Shot in darkly nostalgic images, Lone Scherfig’s densely atmospheric film tells the moving story of a number of people in crisis. A modern urban fairy tale about love, hope and a new beginning set against the backdrop of harsh social conditions. with Zoe Kazan, Andrea Riseborough, Tahar Rahim, Caleb Landry Jones, Jay Baruchel.
