- Biography of the writer Bertolt Brecht (1898-1956), presented in a mixture of fiction and documentary. Heinrich Breloer portrays the icon of political theatre as someone with a lust for life who was also a vulnerable, deeply torn individual. with Burghart Klaußner, Tom Schilling, Adele Neuhauser, Trine Dyrholm, Mala Emde, Franz Hartwig, Friederike Becht, Ernst Stötzner, Lou Strenger.
