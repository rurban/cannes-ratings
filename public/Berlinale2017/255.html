- C: Sergio Morosini, Shine Marx, Domingos Marengula, Chai Fonacier, DP: Joaquin Neira, Julien Guillery, Eduardo Williams, AR/BR/PT 2016, 100 min., Spanish-Portuguese-Cebuano O/U – DP Buenos Aires. Exe, 25 years old, has just lost his job and is not looking for another one. Online, he meets Alf, a boy from Mozambique who is also bored with his job and who is about to follow Archie, another boy who has run away into the jungle. The periphery becomes the center. Humans are everywhere in this trajectory of movement across three continents that shifts seamlessly between spaces and perspectives. The lines are blurred, organic and man-made may not be told apart, melting into a searing, searching gaze.   A. S.
